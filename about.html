छत्रपती ग्रामविकास बहुउद्देशीय संस्था (अखिल भारतीय)

Our Legacy of Service (आमचा सेवा प्रवास)

For **more than 20 years**, Chhatrapati Gramvikas Bhauddeshiya Sanstha has been actively driving change at a national scale. Our core goal is guiding Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), women, youth, and senior citizens toward an inclusive, educated, and self-reliant India.
